A 25-Year Journey

From defense systems to boardrooms to building the future

I started as a digital logic designer and program manager at the Department of Defense. Understanding how systems work at their most fundamental level — that's been the foundation of everything since.

From those early days, I moved into the private sector. I served as Chief of Staff to the CEO at Intel. I shaped technical vision and partner-led growth at global scale as CTO for Global Partner Solutions at Microsoft, leading multi-billion-dollar platforms and global partner ecosystems.

Today, I help executive teams and boards translate complex technology into clear strategic choices. My perspective is grounded in enterprise stewardship, long-term value creation, and effective governance.
